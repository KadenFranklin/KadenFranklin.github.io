Satellites:

Ever since I was young, I have always been fascinated by the stars. Not just the stars for that matter, but all celestial bodies. The Moon, the planets, the constellations, the ISS, and more recently StarLink Satellites. I have vague memories of my mother waking me up in the early hours of the morning to watch comets pass. Even travelling hundreds of miles to watch the "Great American Lunar Eclipse" a couple years ago. I made this site as a small tribute to my love for the heavenly bodies and humans everlasting endevour to touch reach the stars.
